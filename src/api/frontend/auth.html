DermAI Policies

Terms & Conditions

Creating an account means you accept the operational terms below without a separate checkbox.

  • You allow DermAI to store submitted skin images, prediction metadata, and optional feedback comments for model improvement and future retraining.
  • Your submitted images and account-linked data remain private to DermAI and are not intended to be shared with third parties as part of normal application usage.
  • This application is fully AI-driven and does not include manual dermatologist verification before showing a prediction.
  • Predictions, Grad-CAM explanations, and advisory notes are assistive outputs only and must not replace professional medical diagnosis.
  • You should upload only images you are authorized to share and avoid any content that violates privacy, safety, or applicable regulations.

User manual highlights for DermAI:

  1. Create an account, verify the OTP sent to your email, and log in to access the protected application.
  2. Upload a dermoscopic image in JPG, PNG, or WebP format and start the analysis workflow.
  3. Review the predicted class, risk badge, class confidence bars, Grad-CAM explanation, symptoms, advisory text, and medical disclaimer.
  4. Submit thumbs up or thumbs down feedback and optionally leave a written comment to improve future system performance.
  5. Use the profile section to review your account details and recent prediction history.
